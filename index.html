Why SymptoMap?

EXPERT LOGIC SCAFFOLD The platform leads users through a structured "this-or-that" clinical algorithm that mimics how expert clinicians think about things to make complicated assessments easier
Evidence-based Verification At the end of each clinical pathway, there is a distilled summary of gold-standard imaging (MRI/X-ray) and laboratory markers that can be used to confirm clinical hypotheses with objective data.
Differential Filtering Engine The website serves as a digital filter, methodically filtering out “noise” in order to arrive at a potential pathological classification that is most likely true.
SymptoMap is an educational platform for learning and revision only. It does not replace professional medical education or clinical judgment.
