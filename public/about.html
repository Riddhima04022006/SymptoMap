About SymptoMap

Your Digital GPS for Learning Assessment

The Vision

Undergraduate physiotherapy students find themselves suffering from "analysis paralysis" while dealing with complex symptoms associated with patients due to the stressful nature of clinical postings. SymptoMap came into existence as a solution to the problem. The platform is designed as "Your Digital GPS for Learning Assessment."

How It Works

Going from being a student to becoming a clinical expert is not about remembering things from textbooks; instead, it is about learning the thought process. This is where the Heuristic Logic Engine used by SymptoMap comes into play. SymptoMap uses its unique engine to help students navigate through clinical clutter and arrive at evidence-based answers through logical reasoning.

Academic Commitment

SymptoMap is strictly a learning and research oriented website. The platform serves to assist students in studying and revising so that they become competent and confident clinicians of the future.
